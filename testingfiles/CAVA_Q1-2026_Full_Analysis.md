## Executive Summary — The View

CAVA delivered a clean beat across every Street line — revenue +4.5%, EBITDA +8.7%, EPS and comps well ahead — into a soft, promotional QSR tape. The 9.7% comp was traffic-led (6.8% of it), so this is share gain through execution, not price or macro. Management raised FY26 comp (midpoint +150bps to 5.5%) and EBITDA guidance while barely touching margin guidance — a deliberately conservative posture multiple desks call beatable. The print de-risks the growth algorithm; valuation (~58x EBITDA) is now the debate.

**The three things that matter:** (1) traffic-led re-acceleration (9.7% vs Street 6.2%, ahead of the ~8% buy-side whisper); (2) the honeymoon-fade bear was dismantled (2024 class still positive at 18 months, productivity >100%, Blair TAM ~2,000 units); (3) the raise reads conservative (mid 5.5% vs a ~10% run-rate). **Risks:** ~58x valuation, flat RLM with H2 salmon/energy headwinds, an implied H2 deceleration, and unresolved salmon permanence.

## Task 5: Earnings Call — Management Discussion & Q&A

**Management discussion (prepared remarks):**
- Headline: Revs +32% $438M; NI $23.6M vs $25.7M (tax + D&A); Adj EBITDA +38% $61.7M.
- SSS quality: 9.7% on 6.8% traffic; *"a clear industry leader… meet the moment for the modern consumer."*
- Footprint: 20 NROs → 459 (+20.2%); Midwest (Cincinnati/St. Louis/Columbus); 2026 cohort ≥2025; honeymoon NOT fading (2024 class + at 18mo).
- Salmon: national launch (first seafood; ~100bps RLM headwind, "penny-profit neutral"); White Sweet Potato drove frequency + COGS leverage; shrimp in Nashville market test.
- Data/AI: CAVA Core + CAVA Current live ("decade of data transformation"); predictive prep/labor ahead. AGM role >50% of higher-volume stores.
- Liquidity: zero debt, $403M cash, $150M undrawn revolver; OCF $64.1M, FCF $15.5M (vs $2.7M PY).

**Q&A — synthesized themes:**
- **SSS sustainability** *(analyst):* Q2 ~Q1 (~9.7%), above guide; buffer for tough comps + macro.
- **Margin mechanics** *(analyst):* RLM flat = 3P-mix (profit-neutral) + 2% wage + H2 energy; Other OpEx +80bps; won't take price.
- **Honeymoon** *(BofA/Baird):* 2024 class outpacing at 18mo — the fade-bear refuted.
- **SSS breadth** *(TD Cowen):* all regions/vintages/income; lower-income outperforming (k-shaped bridge); no Iran-conflict hit.
- **Salmon permanence** *(Morgan Stanley):* runs ≥4Q, NOT committed everyday — stays open.
- **Pricing power** *(Citi):* would NOT take price; AB1228 precedent — chose not to price, traffic outperformed.
- **Catering / capital** *(JPM):* Houston test → 2nd market; $400M cash, highest use = NRO (gated by leader pipeline).
